Welcome to ARIES LAB

The Applied Research in Software Engineering Laboratory (ARIES LAB) aims to conduct quality academic investigations that bring relevant contributions to the literature and software industry.

    We are a collaborative research group.

    ARIES LAB is a research team at the Institute of Computing at Federal University of Bahia (UFBA) together with the Federal University of Lavras (UFLA) and the State University of Bahia (UNEB).

    Research Interest

    Our research interest is related to the following topics:

    Empirical Software Engineering

    Involves the scientific use of quantitative and qualitative data to understand and improve the software.

    Software Testing

    Searches Related to Software Testing Processes with the aim of improving the quality of the software.

    Software Evolution

    Area of Software Engineering that investigates the continual development of systems and their changings.

    Mining Software Repositories

    Analyzes the rich data available in software repositories, to uncover interesting about software systems, projects, and software engineering.

    Sustainable Software Engineering

    An emerging discipline with principles, philosophies, and competencies to define, develop, and run sustainable software applications.
